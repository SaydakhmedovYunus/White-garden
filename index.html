The company LLC "RUSTAM" was established in 1993. Its main activity is the production of vegetable oil from oilseed crops grown in the Republic of Uzbekistan, including peanut, sunflower, safflower, sesame, flax, rapeseed, and others. In addition to vegetable oil, the company also produces oil cake for feeding animals, poultry, and fish. The enterprise was initially organized to facilitate a quick transition from one type of raw material to another. All products manufactured by LLC "RUSTAM" are GMO-free, and the company maintains constant quality control; the products are certified. Since 2023, the company's management has decided to expand its scope of activity by starting to export peanuts, beans, and mung beans (green gram).
